Things to do first

1. Unlocking will format your device, so backup your important data.
2. Go to Settings→About Phone→ and tap on Build Number 7 times to enable Developer Options.
3. Go to Settings→Additional Settings→Developer Options and enable it.
4. Enable USB Debugging and tap on Unlock Status. Add your Mi Account and bind it to device.

Requirements

1. A Computer with Internet connection & USB cable
2. Mi Unlock Tool(to unlock bootloader)
3. ADB & Device Driver Installer
4. TWRP(Custom Recovery)
5. Magisk Zip(To root your device)

Steps to Unlock Bootloader

1. Install Mi Unlock Tool, ADB and device Driver.
2. Connect your phone to pc via USB cable.
3. Go to Fastboot mode by pressing Power & Volume Down button.
4. Open Mi Unlock Tool and login with your mi account used in phone.
5. After login, click on UNLOCK and confirm.
6. Device will be formatted and will take around 5-6 mins to boot.
7. Congratulations!!!!!
