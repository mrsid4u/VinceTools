Make a Backup of your current ROM

1. Reboot to recovery.
2. Click on backup.
3. Select System, Data, Vendor and Boot, now backup the selected partition.
4. Also backup your important files, sometimes you can face encryption problem.
5. Now you can flash any other ROM.B

How to flash a ROM?

1. Download the ROM.
2. Download Gapps if not included.
3. Remove your lockscreen security to avoid encryption problem.
4. Reboot to recovery by pressing Power + VolumeUp button.
5. Click on wipe tab and swipe to wipe normally.
6. Now go to install tab and locate downloaded ROM and Flash.
7. Flash Gapps if not included in the ROM.
8. Flash Magisk.zip if want to root your phone.
9. Reboot to system.
10.Congratulations!!!

How to flash a Kernel?

1. Download a Kernel zip file.
2. Reboot to recovery.
3. Backup your current Boot(Kernel).
4. Flash the downloaded kernel.zip
5. Reboot System.
